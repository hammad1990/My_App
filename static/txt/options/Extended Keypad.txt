The unit can be equipped with extended keypad to be installed at suitable place at site.Through this keypad , user can do the following:
1-Start/Stop the unit.
2-Change Unit setpoint.
3-Check all alarms and reset them.
4-Check all sensors readings.
The keypad is supplied with 1.5m cable as standard from the factory.Anyway, the maximum cable distance is 50mtr and the cable type is RJ45-Ethernet.
