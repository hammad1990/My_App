The Condenser fans will be switched on upon compressors cut in. Part of the condenser fans will still on as long as one of the compressors are on. The other part will be switched on as long as ambient air temperature is above 15.7 deg, once 15.7 deg reached, then this part of condenser fans will stop to maintain the head pressure.Anyway, The fans will start again once ambient temp becomes above 15.7 deg considering hystresis.
