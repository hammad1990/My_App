In this mode heaters will start when below conditions are satisfied:
1-Supply fan is started.
2-Room/return humidity reading  is above humidity setpoint( 50%-adj).
3-Supply temperature reading is below reheat setpoint (13c).
4-Supply temperature reading is above low supply limit (10c). 
Unit will make deep cooling by keep the compressors swithced on and then the heaters will start to maintain supply
temperature.Anyhow, the process will continue until any of the above conditions became not satisfied.
